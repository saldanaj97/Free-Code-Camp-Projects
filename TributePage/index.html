Stephen Hawking

01/8/1942 - 03/14/2018

Below is a condensed timeline of Stephen Hawking's life:

  • 1942 - Born in Oxford, United Kingdom
  • 1952 - Enrolls at St. Albans School
  • 1959 - Begins studying physics at Oxford
  • 1962 - Starts graduate work at Cambridge
  • 1963 - Diagnosed with ALS
  • 1965 - Marries Jane Wilde
  • 1966 - Receives his PhD at age 23
  • 1967 - First son, Robert is born
  • 1970 - Discovers the second law of black hole dynamics and his Daughter, Lucy, is born
  • 1974 - Elected as a fellow of the Royal Security
  • 1975 - Publishes paper on Hawking Radiation
  • 1979 - Second Son, Timothy, is born and becomes Lucasian professor at Cambridge
  • 1983 - Publishes the Hartle-Hawking State model
  • 1988 - A Brief History of Time is published
  • 1995 - Divorces his wife and marries Elaine Mason
  • 2001 - The Universe in a Nutshell is published
  • 2005 - Publishes Information Loss in Black Holes
  • 2006 - Divorces Elaine Mason
  • 2009 - Receives the Presidential Medal of Freedom
  • 2010 - The Grand Design is published
  • 2011 - Participates in trial for iBrain
  • 2018 - Dies peacefully at home
